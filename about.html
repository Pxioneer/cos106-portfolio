About

A designer who got curious
about how things break.

I've been freelancing as a graphic and UI/UX designer since 2021, running Atlas Creative out of Ikorodu, Lagos. Somewhere along the way I got pulled into cybersecurity — not as a pivot, but as the other half of the same question: does this actually work the way it's supposed to?

Education

Background

Currently pursuing a B.Sc in Cybersecurity at Miva Open University, studied remotely alongside running Atlas Creative full-time. Coursework covers web technologies, networks, and systems security — the practical complement to years of self-taught design and front-end work.

Where this is going

Career aspirations

Long-term, the goal is to keep both sides active: grow Atlas Creative into a small studio handling brand and product design end-to-end for Lagos businesses, while building a security practice — application penetration testing and remediation reporting — that draws on the same systems-thinking design already requires. Fluency in both means fewer blind spots in either.
